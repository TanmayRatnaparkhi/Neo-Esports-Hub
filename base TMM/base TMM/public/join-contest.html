How to Join Custom Room in Battlegrounds Mobile India These are the steps to join the custom room in BGMI.

Step-1 Open Battlegrounds Mobile India on your smartphone On the bottom-left side click on Select Mode

Step-2 From the left panel, select Room

Step-3 At the top of the screen, select Room or Tournament Room  Now, Enter the room ID and click on Enter

Step-4 If the custom room has a password, then enter the password (If there is no password, then you will directly enter into the room)

Step-5 In the Room, you will be assigned to a random team (only for squad matches) You can change the team by tapping on the blank box under the desired team to you want to join

That’s it! You have successfully joined the custom Room in BGMI.
